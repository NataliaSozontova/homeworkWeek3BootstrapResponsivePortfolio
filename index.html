About Me

Hello!
My name is Natalia. I would like to welcome you to my portfolio website. Since my school time, I was always in love with math and physics, and this led me to get my Master's in Network Engineering. After graduation, I wanted to start a career in the IT field. First, I tried to become a Network Engineer or to get into Cyber Security, but life played differently than I planned it, and I decided to dive into the coding world and learned Java. I am currently working as an SDET, and I am writing the automation test scripts for varios Web applications. I also like to learn something new, and the GW Bootcamp program has a list of tools I don't know yet.

If you would like to contact me feel free to send me a message in the contact page or find me in social media by clicking on the icons.

My life motto -
